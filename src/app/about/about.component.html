ABOUT FRAUD CHECK

WHAT IS FRAUD CHECK ABOUT

Fraud Check is a web based platform that keeps records of fraudulent activities in Nigeria and fraudsters of previous scams being reported by the populace and also provides a user friendly AI (Artificial Intelligence) platform to help people understand how to go about online and offline transactions to keep them away from fraud.

The Objective of fraudcheck is to reduce the cases of fraudulent cativities in Nigeria, educate people on the rudiments of transacting online and offline to avoid fraudsters and to help the security agencies have a better track record in vindicating fraudsters.

In this evolving digital age, internet scam is inevitable, we are here to educate people on how to avoid scam so that the fraudulent rate in Nigeria can reduce.
